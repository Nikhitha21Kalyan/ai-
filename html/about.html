About Our Project

AI Healthcare Fraud Detector

AI Healthcare Fraud Detector is an Artificial Intelligence-based system designed to help identify potentially fraudulent healthcare insurance claims. The system analyzes healthcare claim data using Machine Learning techniques and assists healthcare organizations in detecting suspicious activities, reducing financial losses, and improving transparency.

Our Goal

To build a smarter healthcare claims review system that helps detect suspicious activity early and supports safer financial decisions.

Who It Helps

Insurance teams, healthcare administrators, and auditors can use the platform to review claims with better insight and less manual effort.

Why It Matters

Fraud detection improves transparency, reduces losses, and strengthens trust in the healthcare claims process.

Built for Better Claim Analysis

This project combines data upload, analysis, and reporting in a simple web experience so users can move from claim submission to review without confusion.
